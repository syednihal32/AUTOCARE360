Safety Tips & Buyer Guidelines

  • Always meet in a public place during the day.
  • Verify vehicle documents and identity of the seller.
  • Don’t make advance payments without inspection.
  • Check service history and ownership chain.
  • Inspect the vehicle with a mechanic if unsure.
  • Use official payment modes and take a signed agreement.
  • Trust your instincts—if it feels shady, walk away.
